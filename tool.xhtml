How to Use the Tool

Create an annotation:
  • Click anywhere on the image to begin an annotation.
  • Continue clicking around the object to draw a border.
  • To delete the last point, press Delete or click on the eraser in the left toolbar.
  • To end the annotation, click on the first point (highlighted in green).
  • Select the object's label from the dropdown. You may type to search for a label, but you can only use labels already on the list.
  • Click "Done" to save the annotation or "Delete" to cancel creating it.
Edit an annotation:
  • Click on the annotation's border, or on its name in the polygon list, to start editing.
    • Enter a new name or adjust the properties in the edit box.
    • Click "Done" to save your changes.
    • Click "Delete" to permanently delete the annotation.
  • In the edit box, click "Adjust polygon" to edit the annotation's shape.
    • Drag the green handles to adjust the shape.
    • Right click on a handle to delete that point.
    • Right click on an edge to add a point on that edge.
    • Click anywhere outside of the polygon to finish adjusting and save it.
View the image:
  • Options to zoom: (pick your favorite)
    • Click on the magnifying glass in the left toolbar.
    • Hold the Ctrl key and scroll your scroll wheel.
    • Pinch to zoom on your touchpad.
    • Click on the box in the left toolbar to reset the image zoom.
  • Options to pan: (pick your favorite)
    • Drag the scroll bars on the side of the image.
    • Use your scroll wheel to scroll vertically.
    • Hold the Shift key and use your scroll wheel to scroll horizontally.
    • Hold right click and drag the image.
    • Drag with two fingers on your touchpad.
  • Options to enhance:
    • Adjust brightness and contrast using the sliders in the top toolbar.
    • Click on the brightness or contrast icon to reset that option to the default.
